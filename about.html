About SHABISTO TV

Lesotho's premier digital television network — storytelling that connects, informs, and inspires.

Our Story

Born in Lesotho.
Made for the World.

SHABISTO TV Channel was founded in 2025 with a single mission: to give Lesotho a world-class online television experience. What began as a small streaming platform has grown into the nation's most-watched digital channel(129), reaching over 2 million viewers across southern Africa.

We believe in the power of storytelling. From hard-hitting facts and movie experiences to heartwarming documentaries about our culture and land, SHABISTO TV creates content that matters, content that reflects who people are and where they are going.

Our state of the art studio in Maseru employs over 120 full-time staff, from editors and directors to producers and technical engineers, all dedicated to producing television that competes on the global stage while remaining deeply rooted in Lesotho's identity.
